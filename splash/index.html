irl

irl is an ultra-local live feed of events happening near you, today.

browse spots, create spots, share spots.

Get Started

Get Started.

Add irl as a mobile app

iOS

  1. From app.irl.events, tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen.
  2. Tap Add to Home Screen.
  3. Tap Add.

Android

  1. From app.irl.events, tap the Menu button.
  2. Tap Add to homescreen.
  3. Tap Add.
